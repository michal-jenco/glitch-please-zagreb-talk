# #glitchpleasechallenge2026

Printable / shareable challenge card. Use this verbatim on slide 12, in a post-talk IG story, and as a physical handout if you print A6 cards for the venue.

## The rules

1. **Install Glitch Please!** (Android, Play Store).
2. The app starts up with a **default image**. That's your source.
3. **Glitch it however you want** — single image, series, video, grid video.
4. **Only use the app.** No Photoshop, no other editors, no external filters. You can use every feature in Glitch Please! — palette extraction, "use result as source," live editors, video export, all of it — but nothing outside the app.
5. **Post on Instagram.** Tag Michal as a **collaborator** (he'll auto-accept).
6. Use the hashtag **`#glitchpleasechallenge2026`**.

Michal picks. Purely subjective. Taste, surprise, and uses of the app's unique features (recursive glitching / "use result as source", palette work, video loops) will all count.
